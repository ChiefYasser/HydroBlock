Our Approach

HydroBlock's Concept

HydroBlock transforms buildings and neighborhoods into smart recycled water sharing networks. We capture greywater (showers, sinks, laundry), filter it, and store it locally.

This creates a collective resource distributed via a blockchain-inspired cooperative network — saving potable water for what truly matters.

Greywater capture & multi-stage filtration
Real-time IoT monitoring per household
Blockchain-secured water credits & rewards

The Technology

Innovative solutions powering sustainable water management

IoT & Smart Metering

Every home has a smart meter measuring recycling contributions in real-time. Track your water usage instantly with precision.

Blockchain Ledger

A transparent, decentralized ledger that tracks water credits and rewards, ensuring secure peer-to-peer exchange.

AI & Machine Learning

An autonomous irrigation system powered by solar energy. AI optimizes water usage for parks and gardens based on weather data and soil needs.
